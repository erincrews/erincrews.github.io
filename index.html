ABOUT ME

I'm a writer, editor, and communications strategist based in Atlanta.

I help small businesses, universities, and nonprofits align user needs with meaningful business goals to craft relevant, useful, sustainable digital content.

Currently I'm a web editor for Georgia State University, where I work with a team of writers, designers, developers and social media strategists to manage the editorial calendar for gsu.edu.

I graduated from Emory University a few years ago with a master’s degree in English, and I recently completed General Assembly's front-end web development program.
